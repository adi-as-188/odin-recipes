Chowmein

Image by Shree Iyer on Unsplash

Description

This stir fried veg noodle recipe is simple and easy to make. In an hour, make spicy and delicious street style chowmein at home.

Ingredients

  • Dry chowmein noodles
  • 2 Onions
  • 6 Cloves of garlic
  • 2 Bell peppers
  • 1 Carrot
  • 1/2 Head of cabbage
  • 2tsp red chilli powder
  • 2tsp soy sauce
  • 2tsp green chilli paste
  • 1tsp vinegar
  • 1tsp MSG
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Mince the garlic.
  2. Finely cut the onions, bell peppers, carrot and cabbage into thin strips, approximately the thickness of the noodles.
  3. Mix 1tbsp salt in a pot of water and let it come to a boil.
  4. Boil the noodles for 8 minutes in the salt water.
  5. Drain the noodles and toss them in a few drops of oil to prevent them from sticking.
  6. Heat some oil in a wok on low heat and add the garlic.
  7. Add the onions, bring the heat to a medium and stir for a few minutes
  8. Add the bell peppers and carrots and stir fry at high heat for a few minutes.
  9. Add the cabbage and stir for a few minutes.
  10. Mix in the salt, MSG and red chilli powder.
  11. Add the noodles and stir fry for a few minutes.
  12. Add the soy sauce, vinegar and green chilli paste.
  13. Stir fry everything for a few minutes.
