Aloo Chaat

Image by Freepik

Description

This simple aloo chaat recipe is easy to make. Within only a few hours you can make Delhi street style aloo chaat at home. This evening snack is crispy, savoury, sour and absolutely delicious.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 Potatoes
  • 1 tsp Red chilli powder
  • 1 tsp Giger powder
  • Tamarind paste
  • Lemon juice
  • Coriander chutney
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Boil the potatoes for 4 minutes.
  2. Peel the potatoes and let them cool down.
  3. Refridgerate the potatoes for a few hours.
  4. Cut the potatoes into triangular pieces.
  5. Heat oil in a wok at high heat.
  6. Deep fry the potatoes till golden brown.
  7. Toss the potatoes in chilli powder, ginger powder, salt, lemon juice, tamarind paste and coriander chutney.
