French Fries

Image by Hans Benn on Pixabay

Description

This simple french fries recipe is really easy to make. In a few hours, make crispy, salty restaurant style french fries right at home.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 Potatoes
  • 1tbsp Corn flour
  • 1tbsp Rice flour
  • 1tsp Ginger powder
  • 1tsp Red chilli powder
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Boil the potatoes for 3 minutes.
  2. Peel them and cut into 6-7 mm thick strips.
  3. Mix the corn flour and rice flour together.
  4. After the potatoes have cooled down, properly coat the potatoes with the flour mixture.
  5. Refrigerate the potatoes for a few hours.
  6. Heat oil in a wok at high heat.
  7. Deep fry the potatoes till golden brown.
  8. Drain excess oil from the fries and toss in ginger powder, red chilli powder and salt.
